About

I originally had studied Product Design Engineering at Brunel University, teaching me the importance of a research-based design process, and why understanding the user was vital to good design.

I've worked on some world-class projects whilst at Foster+Partners from Apple Stores, Government Masterplans to the Future of Transport, assisting with material research and a focus on sustainability and life-cycle assessments to reduce environmental impacts.

It also gave me the opportunity to hone my design and coding skills, through the development of inhouse applications and tools with primarily front-end languages.

More recently, I've undertaken a Masters in Climate Change at UCL to better my understanding of the science behind the changing environment.
